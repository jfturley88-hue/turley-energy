About Turley Energy

Founded by John Turley in 2009, Turley Energy Consultants has spent over fifteen years helping Irish homeowners, self-builders, and developers make better energy decisions.

The logo says it all: mother earth, passing the world on to the next generation below. That's the ethos. Do the job right today so there's something worth handing on tomorrow.

Independent building energy assessors and consultants. Based in Co. Monaghan, serving homeowners, self-builders, and developers across the North East and beyond.
