Our Story

Turley Energy Consultants was founded in 2009 by John Turley, a Building Energy Rating assessor based in Co. Monaghan. What started as a straightforward BER assessment service has grown into a broader energy consultancy, helping homeowners, self-builders, and developers across the North East navigate the complexity of energy compliance, retrofit planning, and SEAI grants.

Over fifteen years, we've worked on everything from simple property sale certificates to complex new builds, heat pump evaluations, and whole-home retrofit plans. The work has changed as the industry has evolved — but the approach has stayed the same: honest, practical, and squarely on the client's side.

We work independently, without ties to any contractor or product supplier. Our only job is to give you clear, accurate advice — whether you're selling a home, building a new one, or planning a major upgrade.

Based in Monaghan, we serve clients across the North East including Cavan, Louth, Meath, and Dublin. We're registered with SEAI as Building Energy Rating assessors and technical advisors.

The Logo

Why the Mother, Child, and Earth?

The Turley Energy Consultants logo has a simple but deliberate meaning. It shows a mother and child alongside the Earth — the next generation alongside the planet they'll inherit.

It's a reminder of why energy efficiency matters: not just for energy bills or compliance paperwork, but for the kind of world we hand on to those who come after us. Every well-built home, every avoided tonne of carbon, and every good energy decision is a small contribution to that bigger picture.

That thinking shapes the way we approach our work — with care, with honesty, and with an eye on what actually matters.
