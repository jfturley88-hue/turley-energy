SEAI Funding

Grant Support for Home Upgrades

SEAI offers a range of grants to help homeowners improve their home's energy performance. As registered assessors, we guide you through the options from first enquiry through to completed works.

Warmer Homes Scheme

Free energy upgrades for homeowners on lower incomes. This scheme can cover up to 100% of the cost of a deep retrofit, managed end-to-end by SEAI with no upfront cost to the homeowner.

Better Energy Homes

Grants for individual home energy upgrades including insulation, heat pumps, solar PV, and heating controls. Open to all homeowners — works can be done in stages or as a full package.

One Stop Shop

A fully managed retrofit service from SEAI — from survey through to completed works. Ideal for homeowners who want comprehensive upgrades without coordinating contractors themselves.
